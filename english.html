what to do

ACTIVITIES AROUND CATALONIA

Girona

The capital of the Gironès comarca. Its well preserved old town has been the inspiration and filming location for popular series such as Game of Thrones! The month of May is Temps de Flors or Flower season and the city is filled with beautiful flower ornaments, which is the perfect excuse to venture yourself and explore the town.

Costa Brava

If you are looking for beer, paella and amazing views this is the place for you. During May, the beautiful towns of Costa Brava start getting warm enough for you to go for a swim or a dive and take in a full mediterranean experience. If you're staying nearby Girona and love to walk or bike, you can plan for a day-trip excursion and cardio your way to a Costa Brava town. There are also public transport options to most of the towns.

Gastronomy

This is paradaise for foodies and globetrotters who enjoy to, literally, take-in the culture. From pan tomaca to sea food platters the choices of meals are virtually untracktable. The Catalan cuise is rich on savoury dishes that have been sitting on the fire for a while, giving the meals a special homemade flavour. The sweets and desserts range from xuixos and popular catalan cocas to crema catalana and flans.
